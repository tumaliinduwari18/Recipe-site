Chicken Burger Recipe

Ingredients

For the Chicken Patties:

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano

For the Burger Assembly:

  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Condiments (ketchup, mayonnaise, mustard, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken Patties:
    1. Mix Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground chicken, breadcrumbs, egg, chopped onion, minced garlic, paprika, salt, pepper, thyme, and oregano. Mix thoroughly until all ingredients are well combined.
    2. Form Patties: Divide the mixture into equal portions and shape each portion into a patty. Make sure the patties are evenly sized for uniform cooking.
  2. Cook the Patties:
    1. Preheat the Grill or Pan: Preheat your grill or a skillet over medium-high heat. Lightly oil the grates or the pan to prevent sticking.
    2. Cook the Patties: Place the chicken patties on the grill or in the skillet. Cook for about 5-6 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C) and the patties are golden brown and cooked through.
    3. Optional Cheese: If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to allow it to melt.
  3. Assemble the Burgers:
    1. Toast the Buns: Lightly toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster for extra texture and flavor.
    2. Assemble: Spread condiments of your choice on the bottom half of each bun. Place a lettuce leaf, followed by a chicken patty, tomato slice, onion slice, and pickles. Add more condiments on the top half of the bun if desired.
    3. Serve: Place the top bun on the assembled burger and serve immediately.

Tips:

  • Customize: Feel free to customize your burger with additional toppings like avocado, bacon, or different types of cheese.
  • Spices: Adjust the spices in the patties according to your taste preferences.
  • Sauces: Experiment with different sauces like BBQ sauce, ranch dressing, or hot sauce to add variety.
